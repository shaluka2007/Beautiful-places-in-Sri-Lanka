🌿 Welcome to Assan Oya – The Hidden Gem of Eheliyagoda 🌿
Discover Nature’s Serenity, Just a Step Away from the Ordinary
Nestled in the lush greenery of Eheliyagoda, Assan Oya is a pristine natural hideout where time slows down, and the beauty of nature surrounds you. With crystal-clear streams, tranquil forest paths, and the soothing sound of water flowing over ancient rocks, Assan Oya is the perfect getaway for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and peace finders alike.

🌄 Why Visit Assan Oya?
🏞 Unspoiled Natural Beauty – Experience untouched wilderness and scenic landscapes.
🌳 Eco-Friendly & Sustainable Tourism – Built with respect for nature and local culture.
🧘 Perfect for Relaxation & Mindfulness – Reconnect with yourself amidst the calm of the forest.
📸 Photographer’s Paradise – Picture-perfect spots around every corner.
